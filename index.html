About Dr. Kisan Nepali

Dr. Kisan Nepali is a board-certified Consultant Orthopedic Surgeon with over 7 years of experience in orthopedic surgery and trauma care. Currently serving at Bajrabarahi Chapagaon Hospital, Lalitpur, he specializes in joint replacements, sports injuries, and fracture care, delivering patient-centered treatment plans.

As former Medical Superintendent (2019–2021), Dr. Nepali led initiatives to expand hospital services, earning an Appreciation Award from Godawari Municipality. He holds a Postgraduate Degree in Orthopedic Surgery from Maharajgunj Medical Campus, where he received the Dean’s List and Medical Research Award.
